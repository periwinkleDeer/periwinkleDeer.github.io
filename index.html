Nibbler

Nibbler is...

An app that connects you to your favorite delicious delectibles in San Francisco with ease. Give in to your insatiable foodie cravings and explore this rich and immersive app to discover new flavors for your insatiable pallet. Can't make up your mind on what you want to do tonight? Get Nibbler to plan your night out for you by finding three fantastic nom-nom's in a particular neighborhod and showing you their locations on a map! Check out Nibbler here!
